Breakout

Score: 0

Lives: 3


Press 'p' to start the game. During the game, 'p' can be used to freely pause and resume the game.

Press the space bar to launch the ball

Use the left and right arrow keys to move the white paddle at the bottom of the screen.

When the ball hits a brick, the brick will be destroyed. Your goal is to destroy all the bricks with 3 lives.
