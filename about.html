About the Site

qpwoeirut.com was created by Jonathan and Stanley. We are two high school students who are interested in computer science and programming. The idea for this website came up during the COVID-19 pandemic, since we needed something to do during lockdown.

We decided to look into JavaScript and HTML and began to work on an arcade-themed website. Our site offers a selection of simple games to play whenever you want. We currently have a roster of 6 games: Atari Breakout, Conway's Game of Life, Hangman, Minesweeper, Pong, and Snake.

All of our work is written in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and XML using Visual Studio Code. The project repository can be found on GitHub here.
